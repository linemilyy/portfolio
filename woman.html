WomanInc

Overview

For one of our bootcamp assignments, we were instructed to redesign an existing webpage to improve the overall user experience when interacting with the webpage. As my team an I were browsing through some non-profit organizations, we came across WomanInc.

The current WomanInc website lacks hierarchy, making it hard for users to easily find information in a timely manner. For our project, we proposed to redesign the layout of key web pages based on findings through our user tests. We restructured the information architecture and hierarchy after rounds of card sorting and design testing. The overall goal from our project was to achieve a design that would help women navigate smoothly through Womaninc.org with speed.

    Prior to our interviews, we created a proto persona to guide us into finding the proper canidates for interviews.

    Through our research, we have found our users were having difficulties in accessing useful information quickly due to the typography and hierarchy issues of the website. Also, were in need of a better navigation system so that the users could quickly fulfill their needs.

    User Insight

    During our user interviews, we discovered that users have difficulties quickly accessing information due to the lack of typographic hierarchy and organization of information. Therefore, we believe that by reorganizing the display of information, with an emphasis on hierarchy, the user will accomplish more in a shorter period of time which is important given their potential predicament.

    Then with our interview findings, we created a user persona.

    Problem Statement

    How might we redesign the experience on the website so that our users would be guided smoothly when they are pursuing information?

    Ideation

    To properly prioritize the most important features, we created an affinity map and conducted multiple rounds of card sorting.

    Card Sorting Round 2

    These card sorting rounds guided us on how the navigation bar should be set and determine the importance of each category. As we did these card sorting exercises, we also referenced other similar websites to make sure that we were heading towards the right direction. Along with looking at other websites, we also conducted a heuristic evaluation for the original website to explore the positive and negative factors of the webpage.

    During our ideation phase, we also created a mood board to help us decide on our color palette. We wanted to create a color scheme that was inviting and made users feel at ease.

    Finally after multiple discussions on infomration categorization and poential color scheemes, we built out a user flow that a potential user would follow.

    User Flow

    After our uswer flow was solidified, we decided to each come up with rough ideas and then coming together afterwards to discuss the strong points in each others' initial iteas to then construct a final layout to build off of.

    UI Branding

    As a team, we wanted to create a bright, warm, and welcoming palette for our users. As most users who would be visiting our page may already be in a stressfull situation, we wanted to be able to create a welcoming space fo them to view.
